This website contains many cat videos that are the recreations of famous movies and shows. Hence, the name is similar to a well-known Netflix :) Go watch some cats on Catflix!

Overheard in New York

Overheard in New York is a website that lets residents of the city submit snippets of conversations they hear while going about their daily lives. Some of them end up being very funny, so you should check it out!
